Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in my registration fee?
Your registration includes admission to all weekend events, a commemorative gift, and one drink ticket per event.
Can I bring my spouse/partner?
Absolutely! You can purchase a guest ticket during registration.
Is there a dress code?
Friday is casual, Saturday evening is semi-formal, and Sunday brunch is casual.
What if I can't attend all events?
Unfortunately, we aren't offering separate tickets for individual events, but you're welcome to attend as many as you can!
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?
Refunds are available up to June 1, 2025, minus a $25 processing fee.
Why are we voting on dates?
We want to ensure the maximum number of classmates can attend, so we're letting the class vote on the best weekend!
